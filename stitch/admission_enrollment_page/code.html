Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

expand_more

For the Fall semester, early action applications are due by November 1st, and regular decision applications are due by January 15th. Spring semester applications are due by October 1st.

Do I need to take the IELTS/TOEFL?

expand_more

International applicants whose primary language of instruction was not English are required to submit English proficiency scores. We accept IELTS (minimum 6.5) or TOEFL iBT (minimum 80).

Are there application fee waivers?

expand_more

Yes, ZhezU offers fee waivers for students who demonstrate financial need. Please contact the admissions office with supporting documentation before submitting your application.

Can I apply to multiple programs?

expand_more

You may apply to a primary and a secondary choice of major within your application. If you are not accepted to your first choice, you will automatically be considered for your second choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply without UNT results? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes, you can apply for conditional admission, but you must submit your UNT results before the start of the academic year or pass the internal university exams.
Is there a dormitory on campus? keyboard_arrow_down
Absolutely. We have modern student housing available. You can request accommodation during step 3 of your online application.
